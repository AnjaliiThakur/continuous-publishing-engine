Apples of Himachal Pradesh

Published: February 2026

Himachal Pradesh is known for its scenic orchards spread across Shimla, Kinnaur, and Kullu. Apple farming has reshaped the local economy and become a symbol of resilience in mountain agriculture.

Beyond economics, apples represent tradition, seasonal migration patterns, and evolving agricultural techniques such as high-density plantation.
