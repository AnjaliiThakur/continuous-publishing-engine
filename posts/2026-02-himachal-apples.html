Apples of Himachal Pradesh

Published: February 2026 · Story of a Mountain Revolution

The apple story of Himachal Pradesh is not accidental agriculture. It began with one calculated experiment that transformed an entire state economy.

The Pioneer

::contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The breakthrough came through :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}, an American missionary who settled in Kotgarh near Shimla. In 1916, he imported grafted Red Delicious saplings from the United States.

He did not plant seeds randomly. He planted commercial potential.

Historical Timeline

1904

Stokes arrives in India and later settles in Kotgarh.

1916

Import of Red Delicious saplings from the United States.

1920s

Successful harvest inspires neighboring farmers.

1930s

Expansion into Shimla, Kinnaur and Kullu valleys.

Today

Himachal becomes one of India’s leading apple producers.

The Modern Orchard Economy

::contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Today, apple cultivation is:

  • A primary income source for thousands of families
  • A driver of rural infrastructure
  • A defining identity of the Himalayan economy

What began as a single orchard in Kotgarh became an agricultural transformation across mountain terrain.
