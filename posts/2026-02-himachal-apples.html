Apples of Himachal Pradesh

Published February 2026 · By Anjali · Software Developer

The apple story of Himachal Pradesh began as a deliberate experiment, not an accident of nature.

The Pioneer

In 1916, Samuel Evans Stokes Jr. imported Red Delicious saplings from the United States and planted them in Kotgarh near Shimla. The altitude and climate proved ideal.

One orchard experiment became a statewide economic transformation.

Historical Timeline

1904

Stokes arrives in India and later settles in Kotgarh.

1916

Import of Red Delicious saplings from the United States.

1920s

Successful harvest inspires surrounding farmers.

1930s

Expansion into Shimla, Kinnaur and Kullu valleys.

Today

Himachal Pradesh stands among India's top apple producers.

Modern Orchard Economy

Today apples drive rural income, infrastructure development, and define the agricultural identity of the Himalayan region.

Author's Note: Apples have shaped not just the economy but the culture and daily life in Himachal Pradesh. Every orchard tells a story.
