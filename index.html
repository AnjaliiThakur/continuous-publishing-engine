About This Blog

By profession, I am a software developer. Beyond technology, I am deeply interested in observing and documenting the environment and communities around me.

This blog is a monthly journal where I write about local stories — from apple orchards and village markets to seasonal changes and regional traditions.

The objective is to combine structured thinking with storytelling, creating a consistent digital archive of regional insights over time.
